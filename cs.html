    Securing Businesses through Cyber Resilience with Zero Trust

    Challenges

    Upsurge in Cyber Attacks, software supply chain attacks, and Data Breaches causing serious Digital disruptions
    Digital transformation caused a shift across the threat landscape.
    On-demand access to ubiquitous data and information platforms is growing.
    Compromised Credentials, Phishing, Malware and cloud misconfiguration were the top attack vectors.
    Digital Supply chain attacks breaches; Compromised third-party vendor, are becoming a critical problem.
    Cyber Risk management has not kept pace with the proliferation of digital and analytics transformations Limited/No Capability to to identify and manage digital risks.


    Measures

    Incorporate Sustainable, Cyber-resilient Ecosystem through Zero-Trust approach
    Effectively managing of digital risks and constituting cybersecurity maturity against Industry benchmarks.​
    Attain borderless security through cloud, security governance, and operations.
    Strengthen your digital identities (Workforce vs. Consumer) through our IAM, Governance services.
    Effectively strengthen your Policy/Data/Access Governance.
    Safeguard your defense through adaptable attack surface management.
    Leverage Proactive Identification and Defensive Strategies against new unknown threats via IOAs and deeper TTPs knowledge of a threat actor.

    Services We Offer

    Strategy, Consulting and Advisory Services

    Our Cybersecurity Strategy, Consulting and Advisory Services provide a structured approach to measure your organization's security posture. It's a thorough assessment of your current business practices against security risks and threats.

    Cloud Security Services

    Our comprehensive cloud security services along with effective security framework, enable customers manage security risks and build security defence in the cloud. Our focused and comprehensive approach combines a versatile strategy with the best technologies, industry best practices and proven security services delivery models alongside GRC to stay ahead of cyber threats.

    DevSecOps and Application Security

    Our DevSecOps Services and Framework provides 'Shift Left' approach towards DevOps Security. This is a collaborative approach to detect operational security gaps in every phase of the software lifecycle process, from design to deployment. It ensures that reliability and compliance are considered at the early stages of development.

    IAM and Governance (IAMG)

    Our SaaS based IAMG Services an outline of critical capabilities required for an effective and comprehensive Identity and Access-centric approach to secure ever-growing ecosystem of Identities and IT assets. IT enables necessary controls to effectively manage the continuing landscape of security risks.

    Governance, Risk and Compliance

    Our SaaS based GRC Service enable organizations emphasize more control over complex and ever-changing risk and compliance dynamics. Our focused and comprehensive approach combines a holistic security strategy with the best technologies, alongside governance, risk, and compliance to stay ahead of cyber threats including Intensified governance, protection and monitoring mechanisms.

    Vulnerability and Threat Management

    Our SaaS based Vulnerability and Threat Assessment service offers a complete vulnerability management life cycle for finding and remediating security weaknesses before they are exploited and helps with improved visibility to security posture.

    Penetration Testing

    Our Penetration Testing service helps measure your organization's readiness to defend against potential external and internal threats. It categorizes vulnerabilities in networks, systems, hosts, applications, networks and network devices before hackers can discover and exploit them

    Data Protection and Privacy

    Our Data Protection and Privacy Services enable classification of data, policy enforcement, guaranteeing integrity, providing controlled access, audit of sensitive data and users. Through our managed services, we monitor data in real-time and encrypt it to keep it safe from exploitation.

    Cyber Threat and Defense Operations

    Our Cyber Threat and Defense Operation services and framework provide real-time monitoring, advanced contextual analysis, automated incident response, Workflow automation, improved response times and Intelligent Orchestration to proactively prevent, detect, and address security threats.
