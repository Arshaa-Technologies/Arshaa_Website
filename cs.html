    Focused Insights

    Cyber resilience enables your organization to adapt to disruptions caused by cyber security incidents while maintaining continuous business operations. It emphasises on your ability to detect, manage, and recover from incidents.
    Reduce your cyber risk through our practical, actionable, real-world advice and solutions that measurably improve your capability to respond to the rapidly evolving cyber threat landscape.
    Through Zero-Trust principles, we enable organizations build cyber resilience and enhance digital trust.

    Zero Trust is a security framework based on the belief that every user, device, and IP address accessing a resource is a threat until proven otherwise. Under the concept of â€œnever trust, always verify,â€ it requires that security teams implement strict access controls and verify anything that tries to connect to an enterprise's network.
    It recommends organizations to optimize capabilities towards sensitive data identification, strictly enforcing access control, scrutinizing endpoints, enabling real-time threat detection, user behavior analytics and monitoring, building effective security and incident response program.

    It is a proactive and continuous process that helps you respond to change and facilitate continuous improvement in your business cyber risk profile. Risks change with time and circumstances. Hence the need to monitor them and their environments, the implementation of risk treatment plans, the system set up to control the risks and the established contexts and risk priorities.
    Review is a continuous process, and it ensures the plan stays relevant and up-to-date. The inevitable regular repetition of the risk management cycle is based on the review process.

    Cybersecurity mesh is a collaborative ecosystem of tools and controls to secure a modern, distributed enterprise. It builds on a strategy of integrating composable, distributed security tools by centralizing the data and control plane to achieve more effective collaboration between tools.
    Outcomes include enhanced capabilities for detection, more efficient responses, consistent policy, posture and playbook management, and more adaptive and granular access control â€” all of which lead to better security
