    Cybersecurity Maturity Assessment

    This assessment evaluates your overall cybersecurity posture against industry benchmarks and identifies gaps which could be
    exploited by threat actors. It does more than baselining your cyber security posture. It provides enhanced visibility
    to drive cyber security dashboards, helping the C-Suite make sense of your cyber risk profile.

    Challenges

    As cyber threats continue to grow in volume and sophistication, it has become more important to have a clear visibility of your organization's cybersecurity posture and maturity levels. It helps you understand how capable you are to combat with today's most advanced adversaries.

    The Need

    Organizations keen to measure their overall cybersecurity posture or the effectiveness of ongoing cyber security programs; it can also be used as a security assurance activity, either as part of continuous review and monitoring process or to improve security maturity levels to combat against a potential cyber-attack.

    Organizations in need to achieve compliance, regulatory, and legal obligations.

    Approach

    Our Cybersecurity Maturity Assessment provides a systematic and comprehensive approach to measure your organization's overall cybersecurity posture across people, processes and technology stack. It's a thorough assessment of your current business practices against security risks and threats.

    It's a comprehensive assessment that provides enhanced visibility on current state maturity of your organization's capabilities, recommendations with your target maturity (where your organization should be to defend against sophisticated cyber threats), and traceability matrix clearly articulating current state vs. target state.

    The recommendations are optimized to fit your business context and related risk factors and ensuring the same are attainable for you to reach target state. Tailored risk profile.

    The assessment keeps you informed on what you can do to achieve target state and is tightly coupled with NIST Cybersecurity Framework and CIS Top 20 Critical Security Controls.

    Process Workflow

    Assess

    Conduct interviews, workshop and documentation reviews to evaluate the security of your organization's digital infrastructure.

    Evaluate

    The level of cyber maturity per site basis or at organization level.

    Recommend

    A roadmap for improving the organization's cybersecurity maturity.

    Align

    Map cyber practices against industry standards (e.g., NIST Cybersecurity Framework, CIS Security Controls).

    Proactively discovers gaps in your cybersecurity program that put your organization at greater risk.

    Provides customized, detailed recommendations on how to improve your maturity.

    Reduced attack surface; Improved resilience.

    Prioritize security controls through risk-based decisions.

    Assurance (Deliverables)

    A brief executive summary report that summarizes the scope of the assessment, the primary strengths, areas for improvement and associated recommendations noted during the assessment.

    An actionable findings report.

    A detailed report showing your organization's current and targeted maturity levels.
