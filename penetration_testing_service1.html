    Penetration Testing

    Challenges

    As Threat actors leverage sophisticated methods to exploit digital infrastructure and assets; it creates new attack surfaces through misconfigurations, stolen credentials, Insider Attacks and more.This leads to exposure of business sensitive data, digital asset(s) and application to insider/external attacks.

    The Need

    Organizations keen to:

    Proactively strengthen their cyber resilience.

    Reduce their exposure to cyber risk.

    Align with leading cyber security standards.

    Insights into our Penetration Testing Service

    It's a comprehensive testing to validate the effectiveness of controls and determine the integrity or configuration of a network, system, or application.

    Our pen test team focused on simulating real-world attacks to determine security preparedness and assess the size and magnitude of cybersecurity breaches. The strategy and tactics were devised based on the cybersecurity report designed by our pen test team after conducting a full round-up study of client's hybrid infrastructure cybersecurity setup.

    We also evaluate the effectiveness of client's cybersecurity framework by using similar tools and techniques used by hackers to conduct a malicious breach. Our end-to-end pen test process is managed and driven by following the best legal, ethical, and technological standards.

    Through our rigorous adherence to the predefined “Rules of Engagement”, we will ensure there are no impacts to your operations or business.

    Based on the findings of our assessment and testing, we make recommendations for specific mitigations to reduce risks and prevent incidents in an organization's business and operational environment.

    Tools we use are a combination of leading commercial and open-source tools.This includes the latest Kali Linux, Metasploit, NMap (or the Network Mapper), Wireshark, Nessus Vulnerability Scanner, burpsuite and more.

    Delivery Methodology and Deliverables

    Delivery Methodology

    Our global delivery approach is to share what we have done, why, when, and how. This will be an excellent continuous test of the controls that our customers have in place.

    Our approach is to narrow down on the key focus areas pertaining to identifying and preventing an advanced malicious attacker or a sophisticated kind of attack.

    To establish this focus, we recommend leveraging MITRE ATT&CK framework.

    This would enable to tune existing controls more effectively, identify gaps where new solutions are required, and establish an overall enhanced capability to detect and thwart advanced attacks.

    Deliverables

    Deliverables (Executive and Technical Reports).

    Executive Summary | High level summary of issues.

    Key metrics and analysis.

    Impact and root cause analysis.

    Action items for remediation.

    Deliverables (Detailed Technical Reports).

    Detailed proof of concepts | Fix information and configuration examples.

    Track fix status of issues | Summary of action items | Issue & Fix Tracker.

    Post Remediation strategy to maintain continuous security posture, review and assessment.
