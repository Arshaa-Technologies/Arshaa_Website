    Cybersecurity Strategy, Consulting
    and Advisory Services

    Our services provide a structured approach to measure your organization's security posture. It's a thorough assessment of your current business practices against security risks and threats. It help determine risk tolerance, identify key business risks, and cyber threat exposures.

    Our team of SMEs, Cyber Security Advisors specialize in risk awareness, compliance, and establishing security strategies aligned to your business objectives.

    We also help plan, implement and enhance your cyber security investments at scale, while delivering insights to fast-track growth and profitability.

    Services We Offer

    Cybersecurity Maturity Assessment

    It's a comprehensive risk assessment of your organization's readiness to prevent, detect, restrict, and respond to threats to digital assets. The assessment evolves traditional cyber maturity assessments by looking beyond pure technical preparedness - taking a 360o view of people, process technology, data and services.

    Learn more
    Security Architecture Review

    It's structured methodology to assess and design a Security Architecture by aligning with Industry standards and zero trust framework to build a comprehensive roadmap for security modernization and transformation. It helps build a robust security posture by implementing a well-Architected Security framework with a holistic Zero Trust approach.

    Learn more
    Security & Risk Assessments

    Our service helps conduct cybersecurity risk profiles, build holistic view of the risk and action plans to address threats and strengthen security. This service is key towards protecting organizations digital assets from modern Cyber Threats. It's a comprehensive and structured approach to identifying, analyzing and evaluating the cyber security risks.

    Learn more
    Cloud Security Assessment

    Our service assesses cyber-risk in your current state cloud ecosystem; provides visibility into the controls, processes, cyber threats, and risks across your deployments. It enables you to make informed cyber risk-based decisions and optimize cloud security investments. It provides comprehensive recommendations to effectively detect, investigate and respond to cyber-attack.

    Learn more
    DevOps/ DevSecOps Assessment

    Its a comprehensive assessment evaluating your current state DevOps/DevSecOps ecosystem against People, Process, Technology and Culture. It provides actionable roadmap and recommendations against best practices and maturity to improve DevSecOps practices, automate and streamline operations, enhance delivery effectiveness.

    Learn more
    Data Privacy and Security Assessment

    Our service enables organizations to improve visibility and control of sensitive data in compliance with regulatory and business requirements. It's a comprehensive approach towards identifying and managing risks associated with personal data. It helps safeguard your organization's sensitive data from internal and external threats.

    Learn more
    Attack Surface Management

    Our service provides a 3600 view of your digital attack surface including actionable risk scoring and security ratings, helps you effectively discover, detect, validate, defend and monitor against evolving array of adversarial threats. It assists you make informed security decisions and prioritize vulnerability remediation based on actionable intelligence.

    Learn more
    Third-party Security Assessment and Audits

    It helps your organization identify, assess and manage risks associated with connected third-parties. It enables effective control of risk and opportunities, enhanced operational efficiencies, sustainability, resiliency, regulatory compliance, and provides better visibility across your connected third-party ecosystem.

    Learn more
    Security Tabletop Exercises

    Its a simulated exercise tailored to your unique environment and operational needs; which emulates exactly what will happen if your business becomes the victim of a cyber attack - which assets might be attacked, what the hackers might demand etc. The objective is to enhance visibility into an attack and enrich an analyst's understanding of an adversary's tactics, techniques, and procedures.

    Learn more
    Red Teaming Exercises

    Our service helps test effectiveness of cyber security defense and incident response of your organization through impersonating a multi-layered cyber-attack targeting digital assets based on agreed upon objectives. It lets you experience a real-world data breach and provides a larger picture of risk posture and your team's readiness in detecting and mitigating the cyber threat proactively.

    Learn more
    Ransomware Readiness Assessment

    This assessment helps you better understand how effectively organization is prepared for a ransomware attack and how incident response plans are aligned for the specific purpose.
